title: "How does this sound create an emotional or spiritual experience?"
---

  Beyond the impressive engineering, the sound of the Chimu whistle may have had an important role in creating emotional and spiritual experiences. The whistle was made to be heard and interacted with, The sounds produced could have been used during rituals or other communal gatherings. Researches suggest that andean whistling objects are associated with funerary practices. The sound could have been made to communicate with ancestors or supernatural forces.  The combination of air, water and sound may have carried symbolic meaning since these forces are closely related to life, nature and the spiritual world in the  andean cultures. The whistle demonstrates how art in the Chimu society could engage multiple senses.
